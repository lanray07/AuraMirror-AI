# AuraMirror AI Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 6, 2026

AuraMirror AI is designed to provide style, wardrobe, outfit, and confidence guidance while minimizing data collection.

## Data Collection

The current App Store build does not collect personal data from users.

Photos selected in the app, voice prompts, style goals, wardrobe preferences, and generated recommendations are processed only for the app experience in this build and are not used to track users across apps or websites.

## Photos, Camera, Microphone, and Speech

AuraMirror AI may request access to photos, camera, microphone, and speech recognition features so users can try style analysis, wardrobe scanning, and voice stylist experiences.

These permissions are used only when the user chooses to use the related feature. AuraMirror AI does not sell this information and does not use it for third-party advertising or tracking.

## Purchases

Subscriptions and purchases are handled by Apple through App Store services. AuraMirror AI does not receive or store full payment card details.

## Changes

If AuraMirror AI's data practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy responses will be updated.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support information listed on AuraMirror AI's App Store product page.
